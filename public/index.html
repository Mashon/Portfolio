Groupboard

Groupboard.co was built in collaboration with two other Wyncode Academy students as our final project. It is designed to be a communication tool between adults and the youth that are members of their organizations. We see it being used to facilitate the exchange of information between coaches and their student athletes, teachers and the members of their afterschool programs as well as many other groups. Our main focus in designing this site was to create a safe environment for updates and announcements that did not require either party to exchange personal information. This project was built in two weeks and we look forward to refining the site and adding additional features in the future.

@rtMarket

@rtMarket was created in two days and presented to a group of friends and family members halfway through the course at Wyncode Academy. I worked on this project with two other students and we are proud of what we were able to accomplish. We had just begin learning how to utilize the Rails framework and amazed ourselves at how quickly we were able to utilize the skills we had just learned to build this with the impending deadline approaching.
